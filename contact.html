Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I report a bug or game issue?

A: Please email support@retrobowlunblocked.com with "BUG REPORT" in the subject line. Include details about:

  • What device/browser you're using
  • What happened vs. what you expected to happen
  • Any error messages you received
  • Steps to reproduce the issue

Q: I have ideas for improving the game. Where can I share them?

A: We love hearing your ideas! Please send them to feedback@retrobowlunblocked.com or post them in the "Suggestions" section of our community forum.

Q: Is there a way to recover my saved game?

A: Contact support@retrobowlunblocked.com with "SAVE RECOVERY" in the subject. While we can't guarantee recovery in all cases, we'll do our best to help.

Q: How can I partner with or advertise on your site?

A: For business inquiries, please contact partnerships@retrobowlunblocked.com with details about your proposal.

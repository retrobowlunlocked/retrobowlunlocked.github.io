Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Retro Bowl Unblocked. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information when you visit our website or play our game.

Last updated: 2024

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

  • Game progress and scores (stored locally in your browser)
  • Basic device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Game performance data to improve user experience

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Saving your game progress
  • Improving game performance
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Enhancing user experience

Data Storage

Your game data is stored locally in your browser using:

  • Local Storage
  • Browser Cookies

No personal information is transmitted to our servers unless explicitly provided by you.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to:

  • Save game progress
  • Remember your preferences
  • Maintain game functionality

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect game functionality.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with third parties. However, our website may include:

  • Analytics tools to improve game performance
  • Content delivery networks for faster loading

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored game data
  • Delete your game progress
  • Opt-out of cookies (may affect functionality)
  • Request information about your data

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your game data and maintain a safe gaming environment. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes through our website.
